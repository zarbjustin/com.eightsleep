Each side of your Pod becomes its own device, so you and your partner can keep your ideal sleep temperature in sync with the rest of your home. Use Homey to make bedtime and wake-up routines feel more personal, with temperature, presence and sleep insights available when you need them.

Add a device, choose Eight Sleep Bed Side and sign in with your account, and your bed sides are found automatically. A bedside widget gives quick temperature and power control. This is an independent community integration that uses Eight Sleep's cloud service and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Eight Sleep, so an account and an internet connection are required.
