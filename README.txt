Monitor and control your Eight Sleep Pod from Homey — temperature, presence, sleep metrics and alarms, per bed side.

Each bed side is added as its own device, so partners get independent control and metrics.

Features
- Per-side power (on/off) and target temperature (13-44 C)
- Live biometrics: bed presence, heart rate, HRV and breath rate
- Sleep insights: current sleep stage, fitness / quality / routine scores and time slept
- Bed temperature and room temperature
- Alarms: next-alarm display, snooze and stop, plus one-off alarms via Flow
- Away mode, Pod priming and water-low alerts
- Adjustable Base support (head/feet angle, snore mitigation, presets) when a Base is paired
- A dashboard widget with a live bedside card and quick temperature / power controls
- Rich Flow: triggers (got into bed, bed empty, sleep stage changed), conditions
  (someone in bed, side on, away) and actions (set temperature, power, snooze/stop
  alarm, one-off alarm, away mode, prime Pod)

Setup
Add a device, choose "Eight Sleep Bed Side", and sign in with your Eight Sleep account
email and password. Your bed side(s) are discovered automatically.

Note
This app uses Eight Sleep's unofficial cloud API and is not affiliated with or endorsed
by Eight Sleep. An internet connection is required.
